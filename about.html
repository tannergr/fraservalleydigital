A little about us...

We are a local Digital Management company serving the Fraser Valley region of the Vancouver lower mainland. We provide digital management services to small and medium sized businesses.

We are here to help you manage your business’s digital brand and digital marketing.

We started our company in 1996 as a software start-up, although we didn’t call them start-ups back then. During the early years, the business changed rapidly, and we became primarily focused on Internet server management, domain registrations, and E-Commerce. We have been an Google Adwords PPC service provider since 2012.

It would be cliché, but we started our company in our garage in Abbotsford in 1996.

This had always been a side business for us. We have now completed our professional careers, so we are now devoting our full-time efforts helping local businesses manage their digital assets.

Fraser Valley Digital is a restart for us, and we are excited about working with local businesses and organizations to help them maximize their digital brands.
